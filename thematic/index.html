Movies are not only used for entertainment, but also for learning knowledge. In every movie, it always conveys messages to the audience. For example, historical movies want to tell the audience the facts that actually happened in the past, so we can learn from history and movies. For superhero movies, it not only shows the image of superhero, but also shows our desires that never happen in the reality, like the power of time-looping and magic. Watching movies is a way to reflect on youself. As movies convey many messages, I want to share my views on different types of movies.
